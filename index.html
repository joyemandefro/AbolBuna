Ethiopian coffee, ethically sourced. From our mountains to your cup.

Our Story

Born in the highlands of Ethiopia, AbolBuna brings you the finest organic coffee beans, carefully cultivated by local farmers who share our commitment to sustainable practices. Every bean tells a story of tradition, community, and the rich soil that makes Ethiopian coffee truly exceptional.

Featured Varieties

Yirgacheffe

Southern Ethiopia

Bright, floral notes with citrus undertones. A classic Ethiopian profile that showcases the region's unique terroir.

Sidamo

Central Ethiopia

Full-bodied with berry notes and a smooth finish. Grown at high altitudes for exceptional quality.

Harrar

Eastern Ethiopia

Bold and complex with wine-like acidity. Sun-dried for a distinctive character and rich flavor profile.
